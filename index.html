Welcome

I am a Ph.D. scholar at the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, IIT Bombay. I am affiliated with the Centre for Formal Design and Verification of Software and the IITB Trust Lab. My research focuses on Formal Methods for AI, with an emphasis on the robustness verification of machine learning models and explainable AI. I develop and analyze techniques for verifying the trustworthiness of AI systems using optimization-based approaches (e.g., MILP, SMT) and have built synthesis frameworks for formal specifications guided by large language models (LLMs) and demonstrations. I am passionate about building reliable and interpretable AI for safety-critical applications.

Research Experience

Vikriti: Glitch Detection in Decision Trees

Developing a MILP-based tool to detect inconsistencies in Gradient Boosted Decision Trees (GBDTs) using Gurobi.

JaNaKa: Learning LTL Specifications

Created JaNaKa, combining LLM hypotheses and formal repairs for synthesizing Linear Temporal Logic (LTL) specifications. View Preprint.
